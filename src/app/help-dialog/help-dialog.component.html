Hello, and welcome to Map!

If this is your first time visiting, don't worry - there isn't too much to learn. Basically there are two main functions of this tool; search for a point on the map using a sentence or phrase, and also generating a sentence when clicking on the map.

By clicking on the map, you are generating two key words, that can be used among a set of 'connecting' words to construct a memorable sentence. Furthermore, when searching for this sentence, the resulting location will be the one you had clicked on previously!
